General Prescriptions & Medications

Fever & Cold

  • Paracetamol 500mg – 1 tablet every 6 hours if needed
  • Cetrizine 10mg – Once daily at night
  • Plenty of fluids & rest

Acidity & Indigestion

  • Omeprazole 20mg – Once daily before breakfast
  • Gelusil syrup – 2 tsp after meals (optional)
  • Avoid spicy, oily foods

Body Pain / Headache

  • Ibuprofen 400mg – 1 tablet after food if needed
  • Muscle relaxant gel for external use
  • Rest and hydration

Diarrhea

  • ORS solution – After every loose motion
  • Racecadotril 100mg – 1 capsule every 8 hours
  • Light diet, avoid dairy
Note: These are general suggestions. Always consult a doctor for a proper diagnosis and prescription based on your health condition.
