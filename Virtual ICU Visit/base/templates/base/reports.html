Request Your Medical Records

We are committed to ensuring that you have easy and secure access to your medical records. Whether you need them for personal use, a second opinion, or a transfer to another facility, we've streamlined the process for your convenience.

How to Request Your Reports

Complete and submit the appropriate form. Requests are typically processed within 7–10 business days. You may choose to receive your records electronically, by mail, or pick them up in person.

Associated Fees

Fees are based on the delivery method and number of pages. Charges help us cover processing and material costs:

  • Electronic records (via CD/USB): ₹540 + GST + courier charges
  • Paper copies: ₹7–₹10 per page + GST + courier charges
  • Hybrid (electronic + paper): calculated as per pages and format
